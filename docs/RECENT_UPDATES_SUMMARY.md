# Workings App – Recent Updates (Last 30 Days)

**Summary for non-technical readers**  
*What’s been improved and what it means for you*

---

## What is Workings?

Workings is the estimation and quoting app for glazing professionals. It helps you create projects, enter measurements, get calculations, and produce professional quotes and material lists.

---

## 1. Look and feel

### Typography and branding
- **One main font everywhere:** The app now uses a single, clear font (Exo 2) across the whole app so reading and using the app feels consistent.
- **Logo and key titles:** The “Workings” logo and a few special titles (like onboarding steps) use a distinct font (Audiowide) so branding stands out.
- **Bigger logo:** The Workings logo in the top bar is larger and easier to see.

**Why it matters:** The app looks more professional and is easier to read.

### Buttons
- **Consistent button style:** All normal (rectangular) buttons now use the same slight rounding (4px) everywhere—Sign In, Save, Create, Back, etc.
- **Sign In button:** The main “Sign In” button is solid black and matches this style.

**Why it matters:** Buttons look consistent and are easy to recognise across every screen.

### Home screen
- **New illustration:** The main home illustration was replaced with a clearer graphic (“Start Estimating Now!!!”).
- **Layout:** The home screen layout was adjusted to match the design, with a clear call to “Create New Project” and “See Templates.”

**Why it matters:** The first screen you see is clearer and guides you to the next step.

### Profile (Settings)
- **Avatar position:** On desktop, your profile picture (or initials like “PC”) is now on the **left** in the Profile section, so it’s easier to spot.

---

## 2. Quotes

- **Empty state:** When you have no quotes yet, you see a dedicated “no quote” illustration and a round **+** button to create one (similar to Projects).
- **Create New Quote:** There is a clear “Create New Quote” button in the top bar on the Quotes screen.

**Why it matters:** Creating and managing quotes is quicker and less confusing.

---

## 3. Projects and measurements

- **Calculate Now in the header:** On the measurement screen, “Calculate Now” is in the **top header** on the right, so you don’t have to scroll to run the calculation.
- **Notifications:** Blue calculation/points messages and the green “Project saved successfully!” message on the results screen now **disappear automatically after 5 seconds**, so the screen is less cluttered.

**Why it matters:** Fewer clicks and less clutter when working on projects.

---

## 4. Exports (PDFs and lists)

- **Cutting List:** Exporting the Cutting List now produces **one PDF** that includes all profiles, instead of separate files.
- **Glass Cutting List:** Exporting the Glass Cutting List also produces **one PDF** with all layouts in a single file.

**Why it matters:** Fewer files to handle and share; one export per type.

---

## 5. Forms and error messages

- **Clearer validation:** When you miss a required field or enter something invalid (e.g. on login, registration, or password reset), the app now shows **clear, specific messages** (e.g. “Email is required,” “Password must be at least 8 characters”) instead of generic errors.
- **Where it applies:** This improved feedback is used on Sign In, Registration, Forgot Password, Reset Password, and can be extended to other forms.

**Why it matters:** You know exactly what to correct, so fixing mistakes is faster.

---

## 6. Login and registration

- **Sign In button:** Black background, clear label, consistent with the rest of the app.
- **Input fields:** Login and registration fields are slightly more compact and have a consistent rounded style.
- **Button style:** All rectangular buttons on these screens use the same 4px rounded style as the rest of the app.

**Why it matters:** Sign in and sign up feel consistent with the rest of the app and are easier to use.

---

## 7. Other improvements (last 30 days)

From recent development work, the following were also updated:

- **Pre-built templates and settings:** Layout and settings screens for templates were refined (quote format, payment methods, PDF export, material prices).
- **Export settings and feedback:** Export settings and the Feedback/Contact screen were improved and (where applicable) made full-screen or easier to use.
- **Icons:** Several icons across the app were updated or replaced for clarity.
- **Payment and subscriptions:** Payment and subscription selection flows were improved.
- **General UI:** Button placement, spacing, and feedback were improved across the app so actions and messages are clearer.

**Why it matters:** The app is more consistent, easier to navigate, and better at guiding you when something goes wrong.
